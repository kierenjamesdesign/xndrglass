## About the Project
This client was tired of his old website, which we designed together using framework and design inspired by SquareSpace. As his company and services have expanded he wanted a redesign to reflect his brand and his work. This website is designed to be aesthetically pleasing, adaptive, and accessible, aligning with his company values. We are working on developing a strong brand presence, as a visual artist and creator.

## Features
* **Responsive Design** Optimised for a seamless viewing across various devices.
* **Interactive UI Elements** Custom button/link styles and hover effects.
* **Smooth Scroll Navigation** Intuitive navigation with smooth transistions.
* **Integrated Instagram Feed** To help increase social media presence.
